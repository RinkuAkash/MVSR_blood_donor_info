CONTACT

CAN:


1.Let others benefit from your good health. Do donate blood if ...
2.You are between age group of 18-60 years.
3.Your weight is 45 kgs or more.
4.Your haemoglobin is 12.5 gm% minimum.
5.Your last blood donation was 3 months earlier.
6.You are healthy and have not suffered from malaria, typhoid or other transmissible disease in the recent past.
7.There are many, many people who meet these parameters of health and fitness!

CAN'T :


Do not donate blood if you have any of these conditions
1.Cold / fever in the past 1 week.
2.Under treatment with antibiotics or any other medication.
3.Cardiac problems, hypertension, epilepsy, diabetes (on insulin therapy), history of cancer,chronic kidney or liver disease, bleeding tendencies, venereal disease etc.Â 
4.Major surgery in the last 6 months.
5.Vaccination in the last 24 hours.
